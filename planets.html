The Planets

Sun

Rules: Leo

The sun represents your core self & identity. It is the main part that describes you.

Moon

Rules: Cancer

The moon represents emotions and feelings.

Mercury

Rules: Gemini, Virgo

Mercury represents how someone communicates and thinks.

Venus

Rules: Taurus, Libra

Venus represents how someone gives and receives love.

Mars

Rules: Aries, Scorpio

Mars represents action, aggression, and how someone takes initiative.

Jupiter

Rules: Sagittarius, Pisces

Jupiter represents growth, expansion, and opportunity.

Saturn

Rules: Capricorn, Aquarius

Saturn represents karmic life lessons, discipline, and structure.

Uranus

Rules: Aquarius

Uranus represents freedom, individuality, innovation, and originality.

Neptune

Rules: Pisces

Neptune represents dreams, imagination, and spirituality.

Pluto

Rules: Scorpio

Pluto represents transformation, death, and rebirth.
